Why Apollo

Named for the sun god.

Apollo was the Greek god of the sun, light, and healing — and the ideal of physical beauty. We took his name because he never asked anyone to hide from the sun, only to meet it well-protected.

Apollo Delphinios

The god who became a dolphin.

In the oldest hymn, Apollo leapt aboard a ship in the shape of a dolphin and guided its sailors to Delphi, where they became his first priests. The shrine took its name from the creature — delphis. The dolphin has been his ever since: a guardian of those who travel under sun and over sea.
